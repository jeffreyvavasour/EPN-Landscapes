Roll Out Lawns

No matter what your need, turfgrass, instant lawns, or topsoil, use our services and be sure of a healthy, thriving beautiful lawn.

More details about this service

Traditionally, Irish gardens have used seeds. However, grass turf takes away so many of the issues previously experienced with seeds. Constant maintenance, watering, weeding, seed washing away in the rain and having to reseed the bald patches all lengthen the long time to having a presentable garden.

our process

A simple process to have a beautiful Roll Out Lawn

Contact us
1.

Preparation

Soil needs to be turned over to a depth of 15cm before laying sod. This can easily be done with a hired rotavator or by digging over the area.

2.

Ordering

You will need to measure your lawn as accurately as possible in square metres. All turf should be laid within 48 hours of delivery to get the best results.

3.

Laying

Find the longest straight line in your garden. This is where you should lay the first rolls of sod from end to end pushing the edges together without stretching the turf. Once the first line is done, you can continue in line with this.

4.

Watering

Make sure to give the turf a full soaking and check that the water has seeped through to the soil. Repeat this twice daily or as the weather dictates. If the grass shows any sign of drying out, water it immediately and frequently.
